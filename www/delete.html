Account Deletion Request

How to delete your TopTube account and data:

You can delete your account and all associated data automatically through the TopTube app or web version.

  1. Open the TopTube app or go to toptube-jijascuas.web.app
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Click on the More menu (the three bars icon) at the bottom navigation bar.
  4. Click on Delete Account and confirm the action.

Once confirmed, your authentication profile will be permanently deleted.

Alternative Method

If you cannot access your account, you can request manual data deletion by sending an email to jijascuas@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.
