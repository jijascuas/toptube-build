Privacy Policy.

TopTube by JijascuasGames — Last updated: July 2026

JijascuasGames ("we", "us", or "our") operates the TopTube mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information: When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we may receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
  • User-generated content: Any data you create, submit, or store within the App, such as YouTube links, educational notes, and privacy settings (e.g. marking a video as private).
  • API Keys: If you choose to use the AI Summary feature, your personal Google Gemini API key is stored locally on your device and is only used to communicate directly with Google's API. We do not store your API key on our servers.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID), language settings, and screen resolution.
  • Usage data: App usage statistics, session duration, screens viewed, feature interactions, crash logs, and performance metrics.
  • Advertising data: Information collected by our advertising partners, including advertising identifiers and ad interaction data (views, clicks).
  • Network information: IP address, mobile carrier, and connection type.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality and features, including sharing your public videos and notes with other users.
  • Local caching: We store your video notes and your Gemini API key locally on your device to improve performance and security.
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts via Google Sign-In.
  • Display advertisements through Google AdMob, including personalized ads based on your interests and activity.
  • Analyze usage patterns via Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how users interact with the App and improve the user experience.
  • Monitor performance, diagnose technical issues, and fix bugs.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each of which may collect and process data independently under their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob — Advertising

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements within the App. AdMob and its third-party ad network partners may collect and use certain data to display personalized or non-personalized ads. This data may include:

  • Device advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, conversions)
  • App usage and browsing data used to build interest-based advertising profiles

You may opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings or visiting Google Ads Settings. For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Analytics for Firebase — Analytics

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymous usage statistics and understand how users interact with the App. Firebase may collect:

  • App opens, session duration, and screen views
  • User engagement events and feature usage
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • Device and OS information
  • Aggregated demographic and interest data

Data collected through Firebase is processed by Google in accordance with Firebase's Privacy and Security documentation.

Google Sign-In — Authentication

We use Google Sign-In to provide a secure and convenient authentication method. When you sign in with your Google account, we may receive:

  • Your display name and profile picture
  • Your email address
  • A unique user identifier

We do not access your Google account password. Authentication is handled securely by Google's OAuth 2.0 infrastructure. See Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Google Gemini API — AI Summaries

We integrate the Google Gemini API to generate AI summaries of your notes and video content. When you use this feature:

  • Your personal Gemini API key is required and stored securely on your local device.
  • Your notes and the video title are sent directly to the Gemini API to generate the summary.

For more details on how Google processes this data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously and implement commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and keep your device software up to date.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information from our servers as soon as reasonably possible. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the address below.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Analytics data is retained according to the default retention settings of the respective third-party service.

You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us at the email address provided below.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

  • Opt-out: Opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Us section below.
