Privacy Policy for TopTube

Last updated: June 2026

TopTube ("we", "us", or "our") operates the TopTube mobile application and website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

  • Usage Data: We may collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Service or when you access the Service by or through a mobile device. This Usage Data may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers and other diagnostic data.
  • Account Data: If you sign in via Google, we collect basic profile information (like email address and name) provided by the authentication provider.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertising (AdMob)

We use third-party Service Providers to show advertisements to you to help support and maintain our Service.

  • Google AdMob: AdMob is an advertising service provided by Google Inc. We use Google AdMob to serve ads within our mobile application.
  • Personalized Advertising: AdMob may collect and use personal data (such as your device's advertising ID or IP address) to provide personalized advertisements based on your interests and location. Google's use of the advertising cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our Service and other sites on the Internet.
  • Opting Out: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For mobile devices, you can also opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings (e.g., "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android or "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS).

For more information on the privacy practices of Google, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms web page: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

4. Links to Other Sites

Our Service may contain links to other sites that are not operated by us, including embedded YouTube videos. If you click on a third party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@toptube.com (or your preferred contact method).
