Child Safety Standards.

TOPTUBE by JijascuasGames — Last updated: July 2026

At JijascuasGames, the safety of all our users, especially children, is our highest priority. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy towards any content or behavior that promotes, facilitates, or involves Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) or the exploitation of minors.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

TOPTUBE strictly prohibits the uploading, sharing, viewing, or promoting of any material that depicts or encourages the sexual abuse or exploitation of children. Any user found violating this policy will face immediate and permanent account suspension.

2. Reporting Violations

We empower our community to help keep TOPTUBE safe. If you encounter any content or user behavior that you believe violates our child safety standards or involves CSAM, you must report it immediately.

  • In-App Reporting: You can report inappropriate content directly within the app using the reporting tools provided on user profiles or specific content items.
  • Direct Email Contact: You can report severe safety concerns directly to our designated safety contact at jijascuas@gmail.com.

3. Compliance and Law Enforcement

We comply with all applicable local, national, and international laws regarding child safety.

In the event that we detect or are made aware of CSAM on our platform, we will immediately preserve the related evidence, remove the offending content, terminate the associated accounts, and proactively report the incident to the appropriate law enforcement agencies and specialized organizations (such as the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children - NCMEC), as required by law.

4. Designated Safety Contact

For any questions, concerns, or to report incidents related to child safety, please contact our designated safety representative:

JijascuasGames Safety Team

jijascuas@gmail.com

The designated contact person is prepared and able to discuss our compliance and practices regarding the prevention of child sexual abuse material on this application.
